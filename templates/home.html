Lumino'US'

AI-Powered Smart Energy Automation System

Project Idea

To create a cost-effective and scalable smart energy automation system that tackles energy wastage in both urban and rural areas. By leveraging computer vision, we aim to provide an intelligent, automated, and user-friendly solution for controlling electrical devices.

About the Project

Lumino'US' uses a custom-trained YOLOv8 model to detect human presence and light states in real-time. Control commands are then sent to an ESP8266 microcontroller which operates standard electrical relays. A web-based user interface allows for manual control and monitoring.

Key Advantages

High Accuracy

Our AI-driven system provides superior accuracy over traditional motion sensors by using computer vision to understand the environment in real-time.

Cost-Effective

It works with your existing standard lights and appliances, eliminating the need for expensive smart bulbs or proprietary hubs.

Remote Control

Access and control your devices from anywhere in the world through the web application, ensuring you're always in command.

Scalability

The modular architecture allows for easy expansion to manage multiple devices, rooms, and even different types of appliances like fans or ACs.
